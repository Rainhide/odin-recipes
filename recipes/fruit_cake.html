Fruit Cake

Description

A Pound of tradition bake, made for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Grannies FruitCake mix
  • Pecan Nuts
  • Yeast
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Put in fridge for 3 hours.
  3. Spray baking pan with Spray oil.
  4. Place mix in pan and cover for 2 hours.
  5. Preheat oven for 360 deg Fahrenheit.
