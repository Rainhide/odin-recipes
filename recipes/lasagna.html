Lasagna

Description

The hot and gooey texture of a right out the oven stack of tangy, greasy, and warming goodness.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Tomato Puree
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Spinach
  • Oregano
  • Parmesan
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Place Noodles inside until firm.
  3. Set aside.
  4. Cut, and blanch tomatoes and spinach with pan of Vegetable oil.
  5. Add Tomato Puree and Oregano
  6. Place Noodles in Baking Pan
  7. Layer each Noodle with tomato mix, cheese, noodle until desired.
  8. bake in oven at 400 deg Farenheit for an hour.
